Who are Moovyn?

Moovyn are a team of individuals who are passionate about bringing the estate agent industry into the 21st century. We've got a combined experience of over 40 years in the property industry, during which time our team have won numerous national awards.

We thought it was time to bring true transparancy and fair, fixed fees to the market, so we started Moovyn!

We're also committed to developing a team of knowledgeable and experienced local estate agents who know the areas they cover like the back of their hands. When you sell your home with Moovyn, you can rest assured that you're in great hands with a dedicated estate agent who'll be there to support you from valuation to completion.

Every member of the Moovyn team is passionate about helping people to sell their home at the price they want. We'll work tirelessly to find you buyers, arrange viewings, and do everything else needed to ultimately sell your home, and you'll have access to a genuinely top-class estate agency service 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year.

Our team are all proud to be able to provide people across the UK with an easier and better way of selling their home. We work hard every day to continue developing Moovyn, coming up with even more innovative ways to provide every home seller and buyer to get the service they deserve.

Our Platform

The Moovyn online platform has been built from scratch using the latest, cutting-edge technology, and specifically designed to address issues we've identified with the traditional estate agency service. This enables us to provide a much more efficient service without the costs of maintaining expensive branches and an unnecessarily large number of staff. We pass these savings onto you, meaning you get a full-service estate agency but without the ridiculously high fees.
