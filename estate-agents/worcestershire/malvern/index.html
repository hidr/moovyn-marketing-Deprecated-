Estate Agents in Malvern

Malvern is a small spa town in south west Worcestershire based at the foot of the Malvern hills. Our agents have years of experience in the area living, working, and selling property.

Malvern is formed from two districts, Malvern Link and Great Malvern. Due to how the town has expanded over the years, many of the areas of the town are separated by open green spaces, making the town feel nestled within the surrounding countryside. The semi-rural feel of the town makes it a great place for families who want to enjoy more of the country.

Like most towns of its size, schools and further education within Malvern is not an issue. Malvern has various primary and secondary schools throughout the town, so finding a school within walking distance is no problem at all. If necessary, are numerous Catholic and Church of England schools available. Malvern is also home to the very highly-regarded Malvern College, a private school for children of secondary school age.

The town is well connected, positioned in between the M5 (to take you to the South West or north past Birmingham) and the M50 to south Wales. The town is very well connected to the nearby city of Worcester, which is only a 20-minute drive away, with regular train services through Malvern towards Worcester, and multiple bus routes. This makes Worcester a popular destination for shopping, dining, entertainment, and nights out for many Malvern residents. This isn’t to say Malvern itself is – the town is home to numerous well-regarded restaurants and bars, and I home to the Malvern Theatres – a theatre featuring everything from family workshops, newly released films, and plays. For more family-friendly days out, Malvern also has own ice skating rink.
