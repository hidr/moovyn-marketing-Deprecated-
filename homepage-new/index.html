Introducing

Your local estate agent (minus the sky-high fees)

Moovyn are just like any other local estate agents, only without the high street office, so we can afford to offer much lower fees. But don't worry - you'll still get a dedicated and experienced agent who'll stay with you from valuation to completion!

The best part is that you can easily see the status of your sale or purchase at any hour of the day or night, just by logging in to your Moovyn account. Manage your viewings and accept offers at a time that suits you.

So how does Moovyn work?

Moovyn makes selling your home as easy as pie

1

Get your free, no obligation valuation

Book your valuation through our easy online system and your local Moovyn estate agent will visit your property at a time that suits you. They'll use their experience and knowledge of the area to assess your home, and will work with you to achieve a sale price that you're happy with.

2

Instruct us to sell your home

Once you've decided to instruct us, we'll really kick things into gear! One of our agents will arrange to visit you to take some beautiful photos and measure up for a floorplan, before using their wordsmith skills to put together a listing that does your property justice.

3

It's sale time!

After your listing's been approved, we'll spread it through the internet, putting your property live on Rightmove, Zoopla, PrimeLocation, and our very own site. We'll also get in touch with any of our buyers who have asked to be notified when a gem like your property comes on the market.

4

Arrange viewings, accept an offer

Part of the magic of Moovyn is our awesome online platform, which is available 24/7 for you to manage your sale. You can use it to schedule viewings, receive offers directly from buyers, and negotiate until you're happy - and our support will be available every step of the way.

5

We take care of the rest!

Unlike some estate agents, we promise to stick with you and hold your hand right up until contracts are exchanged, and you can always use your Moovyn account to check the status of your sale. Your dedicated Moovyn estate agent will always be just a call or message away.
