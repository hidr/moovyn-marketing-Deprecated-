What makes Moovyn different?

Everyone who sells or buys through Moovyn benefits from a range of features that put us head and shoulders above high street estate agents. From our purpose-built online platform to our dedicated local Moovyn estate agent and our awesome support team, we're confident that we can offer you the most complete service possible.

Lovely local estate agents

Moovyn's team of estate agents are based in the areas they represent, so they know exactly what it's like to buy, sell, and live in your region. We're only hire estate agents who are true advocates of their areas, and have the experience and local knowledge to offer our customers the best service possible.

Our agents have chosen to represent places they really love, and work hard to ensure they are genuine experts when it comes to their local markets.

Photography

First impressions are incredibly important, and potential buyers will often make snap decisions purely based on the photos included in a property's listing.

We think taking great photos that truly showcase your property's charm is key to persuading people to come and view it, which is why we take professional pictures using specialist wide-angle lenses. There'll be no low-resolution mobile phone photos on our watch! We'll send you the photos before the listing goes live so you can make sure you're happy.

Floorplans

When you instruct us to sell your house, we'll visit you to take measurements so we can draw up floorplans. Buyers love listings with these, as they help them to understand the space and layout of the property - this can be vital in helping them decide whether or not they wish to arrange a viewing.

Don't just take our word for it - research has shown that some buyers would rule out a property that might be suitable simply because the listing didn't include a floorplan.

Energy Performance Certificate (EPC’s)

EPCs provide an assessment of your property's energy efficiency, giving a rating from A (very efficient) to G (inefficient). This gives potential buyers a rough idea of how much it might cost to heat the property, and is a legally required document. If an EPC was produced for your property in the last 10 years, this will still be valid unless you've made any modifications; otherwise, you'll need a new EPC as soon as your property is added to the market.

Moovyn are happy to provide you with a legally-compliant EPC - this costs just £120 including VAT. Your agent will talk to you about whether or not you need one, and arrange for this as part of our listing process if required.

Online listings

As part of our service, we'll make sure your property is listed on lots of sites, including Rightmove, Zoopla, PrimeLocation, Moovyn, and many more. This ensures your property will be seen by as many people as possible, increasing the chances of finding the perfect buyer.

We take a lot of pride in the quality of our listings, providing professional photos, floorplans, and well-written descriptions, because it's important to us that we do everything we can to market your property to the best of our ability.

Viewings at a time that suits you

We've been in the estate agency business for long enough to know how stressful it can be for sellers and potential buyers to agree on a viewing time that works for everyone - and this isn't often made any easier by having to arrange everything via your estate agent.

Moovyn's platform puts the power in your hands, allowing sellers to list their availability so buyers can request viewings online at any time (and not just when the estate agent's office is open). We believe that viewings should fit in with your schedule, rather than the other way around.

ID checks and financial qualification of buyers are part of the service Moovyn provides, so you can be certain who you are selling your home to.

Receive offers directly from buyers

There's few things as exciting as receiving an offer on your home when you're trying to sell it! With Moovyn, the offers come directly to you, rather than going through the high street estate agent middleman and waiting for them to find the time to call you.

Transparency is important to us, so we put buyers and sellers in touch with one another so they can negotiate without interference. Your Moovyn estate agent will stay with you throughout the process to provide advice and help guide you, enabling you to accept an offer that you're truly happy with.

From valuation to sale, we're there for you

We pride ourselves on our great support, so you can trust us to stay by your side throughout the sale process. Once you've accepted an offer, we'll work with you to progress your sale and make sure we can get you to completion as quickly as possible.

Moovyn's dedicated after sales team is only ever a phone call or message away, always ready to help, guide, and support you, liaising with your solicitors and the other parties in your chain. Selling your house doesn't have to be a stressful process!
