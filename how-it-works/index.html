Your key to a lower fee

Sell your home the smarter way with Moovyn

At Moovyn, our goal is to modernise the home-selling process and take away much of the hassle that people often experience during this stressful time. We've done this by using technology to develop a platform that you can use to manage your sale. The power's in your hands!

Your own dedicated Moovyn agent

If you sell your home with Moovyn, you'll have a specialist agent looking after you from valuation right through to completion. They'll be an expert in your local area, and will give you everything you'd expect from a traditional high street agent and more: floor plans, an EPC, professional photos, assistance with negotiations, and viewings that you can schedule and manage yourself. Your agent will be supported by our great team behind the scenes, helping you out whenever you need it.

Manage your sale on the go

Moovyn lets you do everything on your mobile

Our platform has been designed with convenience in mind, which is why you can use your online Moovyn account to list your home, get live updates on how your sale is progressing, arrange viewings when it suits you, and get notifications for offers and feedback. You can also chat to one of our friendly team whenever you need a helping hand.

Verified and Genuine

We visit all of our properties and ID check all of our sellers so that you can view, offer and buy through Moovyn with confidence.

We make sure that buyers are legitimate. Our team will do their best to check that people who wish to view your property are in a position to buy it.

So how does Moovyn work?

Moovyn makes selling your home as easy as pie

1

Get your free, no obligation valuation

Book your valuation through our easy online system and your local Moovyn estate agent will visit your property at a time that suits you. They'll use their experience and knowledge of the area to assess your home, and will work with you to achieve a sale price that you're happy with.

2

Instruct us to sell your home

Once you've decided to instruct us, we'll really kick things into gear! One of our agents will arrange to visit you to take some beautiful photos and measure up for a floorplan, before using their wordsmith skills to put together a listing that does your property justice.

3

It's sale time!

After your listing's been approved, we'll spread it through the internet, putting your property live on Rightmove, Zoopla, PrimeLocation, and our very own site. We'll also get in touch with any of our buyers who have asked to be notified when a gem like your property comes on the market.

4

Arrange viewings, accept an offer

Part of the magic of Moovyn is our awesome online platform, which is available 24/7 for you to manage your sale. You can use it to schedule viewings, receive offers directly from buyers, and negotiate until you're happy - and our support will be available every step of the way.

5

We take care of the rest!

Unlike some estate agents, we promise to stick with you and hold your hand right up until contracts are exchanged, and you can always use your Moovyn account to check the status of your sale. Your dedicated Moovyn estate agent will always be just a call or message away.
