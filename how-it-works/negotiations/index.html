Negotiations

At Moovyn we always want you to get the best possible result when selling your property. Transparency is incredibly important to us, so if you sell your house with us you'll find no barriers between the buyer and seller, no middlemen and no underhand tactics during negotiations.

Once an offer is made on your property it comes straight to you through Moovyn, so you have the power to accept, reject and negotiate offers directly with potential buyers at any time of the day or night. No waiting around for middlemen to find the time to update you!

When you receive an offer, we'll make sure your potential buyers are actually in a position to go through with the purchase, and if there are any awkward conversations you don't want to have, just let us know and we'll take care of it.

It's entirely up to you whether you want to accept or decline any offers you receive, but your Moovyn estate agent will always be available for support and advice should you need it. We're happy to help!
